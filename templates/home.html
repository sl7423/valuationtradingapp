Welcome to the Trading Platform!

This application is a model that calculates relative portfolio metrics for a given portfolio against a market benchmark. The model calculates a fair value of each stock on a monthly basis, and compares that value against the benchmark. If the fair value is lower than the market value, then we sell the stock, otherwise, the stock is kept. In this version, we keep around 10 stocks and sell the stock with the largest difference between market value and fair value.
